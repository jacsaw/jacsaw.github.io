Child Tax Credit

What is CTC?

The IRS's Child Tax Credit provides financial relief to families by offering up to $2,000 per qualifying child under age 17, with up to $1,500 of it refundable. Eligibility is based on income, filing status, the child's residency, citizenship, and dependent status.
